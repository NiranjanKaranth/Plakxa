We bring together serial entrepreneurs who have enviable records of building successful technology consulting companies. Top-notch entrepreneurial skills, global sales and business development experience, domain and industry exposure to deliver high quality consulting services that help customers build successful businesses.

PASS™ – Proactive, Adaptive, Smart, Selling

Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT), also known as Industrie 4.0, is effective harnessing of sensor and machine to machine data by leveraging communication technologies, industrial automation control systems and cloud technologies. IIoTprovides insightful data from any shop, factory or manufacturing floor to crucial stakeholders/decision makers, which enables them to run their businesses more efficiently and profitably.

IIoT can transform manufacturing, because the data that comes from the manufacturing floor enables efficient digitization, reduces manual intervention, provides real-time insights that leads to better quality control, supply chain traceability and efficiency, sustainable and green practices.

IIoT’s ability to monitor equipment and report any unusual behaviors, enables prescriptive and predictive maintenance, fast and efficient resolution by field service staff, energy management and asset management.

As today’s organization move beyond traditional business-related data that typical ERP systems provide and take a deep dive into their manufacturing environment and process monitoring, IIoT has tremendous potential in transforming their businesses digitally.

Plakxa’s consultants come with a rich experience of Industrial Automation & Control, Manufacturing Execution System domains. They derive this experience while working in organizations like ABB, Emerson, Honeywell etc. We blend the right mix of technology and business consulting and provide a holistic approach towards your digital transformation journey.

Plakxa also leverages its network of partners who have excellent experience in development and execution of Industrial IoT, MES and IAC solutions. Reach out to us to know more.
